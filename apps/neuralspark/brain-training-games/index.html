Apps / NeuralSpark

Short practice games, clearly described.

NeuralSpark is a collection of short games for attention, memory, math, language, creativity, and spatial practice.

What NeuralSpark is built for

NeuralSpark is built for short, repeatable puzzles that feel mentally active. Rounds are small, categories are clear, and the app is easy to use for a few minutes at a time.

That does not mean the app promises to improve your brain, raise your IQ, or treat memory problems. It does not. NeuralSpark is a game app with careful categories and quiet design.

The six practice areas

NeuralSpark groups games into attention, memory, math, language, creativity, and spatial reasoning. You can pick the kind of challenge you want, or let the daily challenge choose for you.

The design goal is simple: make each round readable, fast to start, and complete without an account. The app is offline-first, so a weak connection does not interrupt a round.

What is free

Free players get every game, every difficulty, and the daily challenge. Pro removes ads, adds unlimited hints where hints exist, and changes a few surfaces with a gold accent. The core game collection is not locked away.
