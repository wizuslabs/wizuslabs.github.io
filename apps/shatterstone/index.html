Shatterstone

An offline-first match-3 puzzle game.

Coming soon

ios + android · puzzle · offline-first

What it does

Shatterstone is a match-3 puzzle game in the classic candy-style tradition: swap adjacent gems to line up three or more, clear the board, and work through level after level of hand-tuned objectives.

It is offline-first. Every level is played entirely on your device, your progress is saved locally, and no account is required. The game is fully playable with no network connection at all.

There are no ads and no in-app purchases. There is no subscription and no “Pro” tier. Coins are an in-game soft currency you earn purely by playing — they cannot be bought with real money and have no monetary value.

Shatterstone collects no personal data, runs no analytics or telemetry, and contains no advertising SDKs. Your level progress, scores, coins, boosters, and streaks never leave your device.

In the box

  • A chapter-based campaign of hand-tuned match-3 levels.
  • Stars and best scores tracked per level.
  • In-game boosters and a coin balance earned through play.
  • Daily rewards and win-streak tracking.
  • Fully offline — no account, no ads, no in-app purchases.

Get it

Shatterstone is coming soon to the App Store and Google Play. Store links will appear here once it is live.

Privacy

Shatterstone collects no personal data, shows no ads, has no in-app purchases, and runs no analytics or telemetry. All of your game data lives on your device. The only network activity is a brief, optional check for a newer app version, which sends nothing about you. Full details are in the Privacy Policy below.
