Iron Swarm

Iron Swarm: command your tank, hold the line against the swarm.

Iron Swarm is a fast 2D top-down tank arena you can play right here in your browser — no download, no sign-up. Built with Flutter and the Flame game engine, it drops you into tight, readable battlefields where smart movement matters more than twitch reflexes. Roll out across handcrafted mission chapters, hold off escalating enemy armor, and fight an escalating boss tank every fourth round. The game is also available on iOS and Android, but the web build is free to play instantly.

How to play (web and desktop)

Controls are built for the keyboard and take seconds to learn:

  • Move — W A S D or the arrow keys. Your barrel aims in the direction you drive, so movement and aim are one motion.
  • Fire — Space or F. Hold it down for continuous auto-fire bursts.
  • Launch missile — R. A high-damage shot on a cooldown — save it for bosses and tight spots.
  • Activate drone — E. Calls in a support drone to add pressure while you reposition.

On phones and tablets the same actions map to an on-screen joystick plus fire, missile, and drone buttons, so nothing in the moment-to-moment combat changes between platforms.

Game modes

  • Campaign — handcrafted missions grouped into chapters, each scored with a star rating so there is always a cleaner run to chase.
  • Survival — an endless mode where difficulty scales indefinitely and the only goal is to last one more wave.
  • Daily Challenge — a fresh, daily-rotating objective that gives you a reason to come back every day.

Core tactics

Every run rewards thinking a half-second ahead. Use cover and positioning to break enemy line of sight, line up shots instead of spraying, and grab supply drops and pickups when the lull allows. Boss encounters arrive every fourth round and scale up across the campaign — the Standard boss, the Glacier Warden, the Bastion, and the Iron Commander each run multi-phase attack patterns, so learn to read their telegraphs and dodge before you commit. Manage your missile and drone cooldowns so you have firepower ready when a phase shifts.

Fair by design

Iron Swarm has no pay-to-win, no loot boxes, and we collect no personal data ourselves (the website shows ads via Google AdSense, which uses cookies — see our Privacy Policy). Any ads are optional and player-initiated: you can choose to watch a rewarded ad to revive and continue or to double a reward — they are never forced and never overlaid on your controls.
