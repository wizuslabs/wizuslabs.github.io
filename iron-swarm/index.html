Iron Swarm

Top-down tank arcade combat.

ios + android · arcade

What it does

Iron Swarm is a top-down 2D tank arcade game. Drive your tank through tight, readable battlefields, manage your line of fire, and push through waves of enemy armor.

There are six historical missions, a daily challenge, and an endless campaign with multi-phase boss fights. Boss encounters drop boss-chest field modules — pick-ups that change how your tank handles for the run.

Commander Pro is an optional upgrade for players who want it. It is offered as a subscription or a one-time lifetime unlock. The core game stands on its own without it.

In the box

  • Six historical missions.
  • A daily challenge.
  • An endless campaign with multi-phase boss fights.
  • Boss-chest field modules that reshape a run.
