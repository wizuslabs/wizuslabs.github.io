Apps / Iron Swarm

Top-down tank arcade combat.

Iron Swarm is a tank game for iOS and Android built around readable arenas, fast runs, boss fights, and field modules.

What kind of tank game it is

Iron Swarm is top-down and 2D. You drive through compact battlefields, manage your line of fire, dodge enemy armor, and push through missions or repeatable challenge runs.

The game is tuned for readable arcade combat instead of simulation. The tank should feel immediate. The battlefield should be legible. A run should be easy to start and hard enough to earn attention.

Modes and progression

Iron Swarm includes six historical missions, a daily challenge, and an endless campaign. Boss fights have multiple phases, and boss-chest field modules can change how your tank behaves during a run.

Commander Pro is optional. The core tank arcade game remains playable without it.
