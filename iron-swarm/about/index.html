About Iron Swarm

Iron Swarm is a fast 2D top-down tank arcade game built with Flutter and the Flame game engine. You command a single tank through handcrafted Campaign missions, an endless Survival mode, and a daily-rotating Daily Challenge, facing an escalating multi-phase boss every fourth round. It is free to play in your browser with no download or sign-up, and is also available on iOS and Android.

Who makes it

Iron Swarm is made by WizusLabs, a small independent game studio. We build polished, self-contained arcade games and ship them across the web, iOS, and Android. You can reach us any time at wizuslabs@gmail.com.

Our design philosophy

We set out to build a tank game that respects the player. That means a few firm commitments:

  • Skill-based. Outcomes come from movement, positioning, and reading the battlefield — not from your wallet.
  • Legible difficulty. The challenge scales steadily and bosses telegraph their attacks, so a loss always feels learnable rather than unfair.
  • No pay-to-win. There are no loot boxes and no stat-boosting purchases. Any ads are optional and player-initiated, and a one-time premium unlock on mobile simply removes them.
  • Privacy-first. WizusLabs collects no personal data about you directly; your game progress lives on your device. The website is ad-supported via Google AdSense (cookies) — details in our Privacy Policy.
