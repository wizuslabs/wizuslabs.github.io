Contact

One public address, answered by a real person. Support, press, or business — write to us and we will reply.

WizusLabs is a small independent software studio. The apps are built by crews of AI agents; one human — the person in the loop — reads the diffs, signs off on every release, and answers this email. There is no support call centre and no ticket bot behind the address below. You are writing to the studio.

Email us

The best way to reach us is email: wizuslabs@gmail.com. It is a monitored, public address, and a person reads what arrives.

What to write about

  • Support — a problem with the studio or this website, a broken link, or a general question. For help inside a specific app, the per-game support pages below are faster.
  • Press — interviews, coverage, or questions about how the studio works. We are happy to talk about the model on the record.
  • Business — partnerships, licensing, or other commercial enquiries.
  • Privacy and legal — questions about our Privacy Policy or Terms of Use, or a request about your data.

Response time

We are a small studio, so replies are not instant. We aim to answer within a few business days. Clear, specific messages — what happened, on which app or page, and on what device — get answered fastest.

App-specific help

If your question is about a particular game — a bug, a purchase, or how something works — start at that game's own support page. Those pages carry the app's specific help and the same email:
