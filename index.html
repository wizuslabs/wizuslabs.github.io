NeuralSpark

v1.0 · neuralspark · web + ios + android

NeuralSpark is a pocketful of short mental workouts — more than seventy small games sorted into six practice areas: attention, memory, math, language, creativity, and spatial reasoning. Open it, choose the area you feel like sharpening, and finish a round in the time it takes to boil a kettle.

Every round is deliberately brief and the feedback stays calm. There are no streak-guilt mechanics, no timers screaming at you, and no hint-store nagging. It is offline-first, so a dead signal on the train never cuts a game short — and the free tier includes every game, every difficulty, and a daily challenge that picks the exercise for you.

It is built for anyone who likes a quick, quiet brain teaser tucked between the other things in their day. We make no promises about raising your IQ; NeuralSpark is simply a well-made game with honest categories.

Sudoku by WizusLabs

v1.0 · sudoku · web + ios + android

Sudoku by WizusLabs is the classic nine-by-nine grid done properly, with killer variants and a fresh daily challenge alongside it. Every puzzle is generated on device, has exactly one solution, and is checked against a full correctness suite before it reaches you — no broken boards ever ship.

Play offline, with no account and no sign-in. A mistake counter is on by default and can be switched off, wrong entries can auto-clear if you prefer, and hints are there when you are stuck. The daily challenge is free for everyone; an optional Pro tier removes ads, unlocks unlimited hints, and lifts the mistake cap.

It is for the commuter, the queue-waiter, and anyone who wants a calm, dependable puzzle without a cluttered interface getting in the way. Sudoku is Sudoku — we work hard to keep it that way.

Iron Swarm

ios + android · arcade

Iron Swarm is a fast top-down tank arena. You drive a single tank through tight, readable battlefields, aiming where you steer, holding the line as waves of enemy armor escalate and a boss tank rolls in every fourth round.

There is a handcrafted campaign scored by stars, an endless survival mode that scales until you fall, and a rotating daily challenge to pull you back. Missiles and a support drone sit on cooldowns, so every run rewards thinking half a second ahead — use cover, line up your shots, and read a boss's telegraph before you commit.

It is made for players who want arcade action with a tactical edge. There is no pay-to-win and there are no loot boxes; a one-time unlock removes ads on mobile. Play free in the browser, or take it with you on iOS and Android.

Manifesto · Agents · Human

How we decide.

1 — Accessibility is a floor, not a feature.

Every page passes WCAG 2.2 AA. Every app is built to be used by a tired person on a small phone.

2 — Longevity over novelty.

We pick tools that will still work in ten years. The site is plain HTML on GitHub Pages for a reason.

3 — Human review, every release.

No commit ships to a store without a human reading the diff and signing off. That is what "in the loop" means.
